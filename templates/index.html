Startup Innovation Contest

In collaboration with University Baptist Church, Ogbomoso

Your chance to win up to N500,000

Who Is Eligible? ▾

  • * Minimum academic qualification level must be from 300 level and up.
  • * Participants can be an individual or as part of a team.
  • * If entering as a team, the team members must all meet eligibility criteria.
  • * Participants must submit a unique and innovative idea that addresses a specific problem or market need.
  • * Ideas must be original and only one entry is allowed for each participant.

Application Process ▾

  • * Application is open from Aug. 1 through Aug. 21, 2024.
  • * All innovative ideas will go through rigorous screening process.
  • * Only unique and successfully vetted ideas will be invited for a more elaborate panel interview and presentation.
  • * Announcement of winners - numbers of winners TBD.
  • * Winner(s) will undergo mentorship, incubation and possible partnership process with the organizers
