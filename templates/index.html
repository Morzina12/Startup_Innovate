Who Is Eligible? ▾

  • Participants must be at least 18 years old.
  • Participants must be residents of the specified country/region.
  • Participants must not be employees or immediate family members of the organizers.
  • Participants must have a unique and innovative idea that addresses a specific problem or market need.

Application Process ▾

  • Fill out the application form with your full name, email address, and idea details.
  • Describe your idea in no more than 150 words, highlighting its uniqueness and potential impact.
  • Agree to the terms and conditions and submit your application.
  • Wait for a confirmation email with further instructions.
